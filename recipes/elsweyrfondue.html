Elsweyr Fondue

Elsweyr Fondue is a delicious and hearty dish popular in Skyrim. Nothing quite beats gathering with friends and family around the fondue pot, sharing the simple joy of warm cheese and fresh bread Elsweyr fondue is unique in that it utilises three ingredients: ale, moon, sugar, and an Eidar cheese wheel. As such, the cheesy flavour is made more complex with the use of blue Eidar cheese, as well as the warm bitterness of ale and a hint of sweetness from the moon sugar.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • 1 bottle of ale, left overnight to go flat
  • 100g gruyere cheese, grated
  • 100g emmental cheese, grated
  • 100g soft blue cheese, diced
  • Brown sugar, to taste
  • Fresh bread, cut into cubes

Directions

  1. Step 1: Set up your fondue pot. Add the ale and sugar, and stir until sugar has melted
  2. Step 2: Add the grated cheese bit by bit, stirring to ensure it melts smoothly
  3. Step 3: Once melted, add the blue cheese pieces and stir until the mixture is completely smooth

The Elsweyr fondue is now ready to eat! Pass a bowl of bread cubes around the table and enjoy.
